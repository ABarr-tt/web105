Welcome to Our Restaurant

At our restaurant, we believe in providing a unique and memorable dining experience for our guests. Nestled in the heart of Chicago, we have been serving delicious and thoughtfully crafted dishes since 1973.

Our commitment to quality extends to every aspect of your visit. From the carefully sourced ingredients to the warm and inviting ambiance, we strive to create an atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

What sets us apart is our passion for wood-fired grilling. We take pride in using high-quality hardwoods to infuse our dishes with rich, smoky flavors. Our skilled chefs utilize traditional grilling methods, ensuring that each item on our menu is a true culinary delight.

Whether you're here for a casual meal with friends or a special celebration, we welcome you to savor the flavors of our wood-fired cuisine and enjoy the warmth of our hospitality.
